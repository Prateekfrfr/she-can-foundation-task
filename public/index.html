Who we are

She Can Foundation is a non-governmental organization registered under the Indian Society Act, 1860. The foundation is dedicated to empowering women and creating a more equitable society through support, resources, training, and awareness initiatives.

The team works with local organizations, governments, and communities so that programs are meaningful, practical, and sustainable.

"Together, we can break down barriers and empower women. There is no challenge too great to overcome when everyone does their part."

Reeta Mishra Founder & President, She Can Foundation
